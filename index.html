English

Tero is a software done in Python that lets you know when someone is in your house (without your knowledge). Right now it works like a very basic surveillance home system that to works needs:

  • IP Cameras that support 'motion detection' and can upload photos to a FTP server. (Any camera supporthing this two features should work).
  • You use Telegram in your smartphone
  • A place to install Tero (the smallest Digital Ocean machines, those that cost 5 usd per month, work awsome!).
